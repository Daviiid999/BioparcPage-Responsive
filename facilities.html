Explore our Natural World

Journeying Through Our Biopark

Our biopark is a place where nature comes to life, a haven for wildlife enthusiasts and conservationists alike. Nestled within a sprawling natural landscape, our facilities have been thoughtfully designed to offer an immersive and educational experience for visitors of all ages.

From interactive exhibits to engaging educational programs, every corner of our biopark is crafted to inspire curiosity, foster a deeper appreciation for the natural world, and ignite a passion for conservation that reverberates far beyond our park's borders.

Upon entering the biopark, you'll find yourself surrounded by lush, verdant gardens that mimic the diverse ecosystems of the world.

Exploring the Wonders of Our Biopark

At our biopark, nature isn't just a backdrop; it's the main attraction. A sanctuary for wildlife lovers and conservation advocates alike, our biopark is nestled within an expansive natural landscape, inviting visitors to explore, learn, and connect with the wonders of the natural world.

The towering trees to the bubbling streams, every element of our landscape has been carefully curated to create an immersive and educational experience for visitors of all ages.

Our facilities are more than just buildings; they are gateways to understanding and appreciation. Interactive exhibits, informative displays, and hands-on activities await around every corner, inviting visitors to delve deeper into the mysteries of the natural world.

DISCOVER OUR AREAS

Savanna

East Bioparc

Step into the heart of Africa within our biopark as you enter the enchanting realm of the African Savannah. This sprawling landscape faithfully replicates the vast and diverse ecosystem of the continent's grasslands.

This sprawling landscape faithfully replicates the vast and diverse ecosystem of the continent's grasslands, inviting you to embark on a journey of discovery amidst towering acacia trees, golden savannahs, and the majestic wildlife that calls this enchanting realm home.

Jungle

West Bioparc

As you venture deeper into our biopark, you'll find yourself transported to the lush and vibrant heart of the jungle. This enthralling space is a tribute to the world's most biodiverse and exotic ecosystems, where life thrives in every nook and cranny.

This enthralling space is a tribute to the world's most biodiverse and exotic ecosystems, where life thrives in every nook and cranny, beckoning you to explore dense foliage, echoing calls of wildlife, and hidden wonders waiting to be discovered at every turn.

Sea

South Bioparc

Step into a world of aquatic wonder as you enter our mesmerizing Marine Zone. This captivating section of our biopark transports you to the depths of the ocean, where a symphony of life thrives beneath the waves.

Explore intricate coral gardens, encounter graceful sea turtles gliding effortlessly, and marvel at the kaleidoscope of colors as schools of tropical fish dart through the azure waters, offering a glimpse into the mesmerizing diversity of marine ecosystems.

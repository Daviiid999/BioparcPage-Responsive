About Us

Discovering Nature in New Horizons

Since its establishment in 1995, this company has been fully dedicated to the design, development, and management of new generation zoos.

Its revolutionary approach offers visitors the unique opportunity to immerse themselves in natural habitats where animals live in social groups specific to their species and coexist with other compatible species, thus replicating natural dynamics.

Through careful planning and meticulous attention to detail, each park created by this company becomes an oasis of biodiversity and learning, where conservation and education go hand in hand.

Environmental Education

The experience provided by these parks goes beyond simply observing animals in captivity; This is a complete wildlife immersion, where visitors can learn about the importance of preserving ecosystems and respecting all the life forms with which we share our planet.

With a firm commitment to animal welfare and environmental sustainability, this company continues to set the standard in the industry, inspiring others to adopt responsible and ethical practices in wildlife care.

Their legacy endures not only in the beauty of their parks, but also in the positive impact they generate on the collective consciousness about the importance of protecting and conserving nature for future generations.

The Approach to Zoos of the Future

Through captive breeding programs and reintroduction projects, the company is committed to playing an active role in conserving biodiversity and restoring threatened ecosystems.

In addition to providing a sanctuary for wildlife, these zoos act as research and conservation centers, collaborating with scientific and environmental organizations to contribute to the study and preservation of endangered species.

Thus demonstrating that the commitment to the protection of wildlife is a shared responsibility that transcends the borders of the parks and reaches the entire global community.

OUR TEAM

Veterinarians

Our team of veterinarians is a group of professionals who ensure the health and well-being of animals through comprehensive medical care, regular check-ups, and the implementation of tailored enrichment programs to support their physical and psychological needs.

They collaborate closely with animal behaviorists, nutritionists, and environmental specialists to develop holistic care plans that promote the animals' overall welfare and encourage natural behaviors. With a deep understanding of each species' unique biology and social dynamics, our veterinarians strive to provide individualized care that fosters trust and establishes..

Scientist

Our team of scientists embodies a collective passion for unraveling the intricate mysteries of our park's ecosystem. Comprising researchers, ecologists, and conservationists, they devote themselves to studying the interconnected web of life within our park's boundaries.

Through rigorous fieldwork, data analysis, and collaboration with external partners, our scientists strive to gain insights into the complex dynamics that shape the biodiversity and sustainability of our natural environment.

Employees

Their work extends beyond mere observation, as they actively engage in applied research aimed at developing innovative conservation strategies and management practices.

From tracking animal movements to monitoring changes in vegetation patterns, our scientists employ cutting-edge techniques and technologies to gather valuable information essential for informed decision-making.

Caretakers

Beyond meeting their basic needs, the caretakers devote significant time and effort to enriching the animals' lives through engaging activities, environmental enhancements, and social interactions that stimulate their natural behaviors and cognitive abilities.

As the primary advocates for the well-being of our animal residents, the caretakers exemplify the highest standards of compassion and dedication, embodying our enduring commitment to nurturing a harmonious coexistence between humans and wildlife.

Future Projects

Get to know our projects

We are excited to announce the development of a research and innovation center in the heart of the Bioparc. This center will become an epicenter of scientific discovery, where we will collaborate with scientists around the world to address pressing problems related to biodiversity conservation and climate change.

To further engage our visitors in the cause of conservation, we plan to create an exciting interactive space where visitors can learn about the importance of biodiversity and the actions they can take to make a difference. It will be a place for education and inspiration, where people of all ages can discover the beauty and fragility of our planet.
